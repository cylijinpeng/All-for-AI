# Research to brief workflow

## Goal

Turn a broad topic into a concise brief with sources, key claims, risks, and next actions.

## Inputs

- Topic or question
- Optional seed links
- Audience and output format

## Steps

1. Clarify scope and success criteria.
2. Gather and rank sources.
3. Extract claims and evidence.
4. Synthesize patterns and uncertainties.
5. Write the final brief.

## Deliverables

- One-page brief
- Source list
- Open questions
