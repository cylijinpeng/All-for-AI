# Literature review skill

## Summary

A research-focused skill for turning a topic, source list, or paper set into a structured literature review draft.

## Best for

- Paper comparisons
- Source synthesis
- Theme extraction
- Research brief preparation

## Suggested behavior

1. Restate the research question.
2. Group sources by theme, method, or claim.
3. Extract agreements, disagreements, and gaps.
4. Produce a concise synthesis with citations.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent citations
- Separate facts from inferences
- Flag weak or outdated sources
